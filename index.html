How to Use This Tool

Enter any number in the input field below, and the bot will classify it according to the number system hierarchy:

  • Natural numbers: Positive integers (1, 2, 3, ...)
  • Integers: Whole numbers including negatives and zero (...-2, -1, 0, 1, 2...)
  • Rational numbers: Numbers that can be expressed as a ratio of integers (includes terminating and repeating decimals)
  • Irrational numbers: Numbers that cannot be expressed as a ratio of integers (π, √2, etc.)
  • Real numbers: All rational and irrational numbers
  • Imaginary numbers: Numbers expressed as a real number multiplied by the imaginary unit i (where i = √-1)
  • Complex numbers: Numbers with both real and imaginary parts (a + bi)
